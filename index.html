Monthly Reports (Department Wise, Example: Sales Department)

Sales Department Wrecker Report

  • Overthinker
    • Insight: Employees who overthink tend to delay decision-making, leading to inefficiencies and missed opportunities, ultimately affecting productivity and team morale.
    • Recommended Action: Implement decision-making frameworks to help employees act quickly and confidently.

  • Pleaser
    • Insight: Employees who excessively please others may struggle with setting boundaries, leading to burnout and resentment, and ultimately decreasing overall team performance and well-being.
    • Recommended Action: Train managers to foster an environment of psychological safety where employees feel comfortable expressing their needs and setting healthy boundaries.

Sales Department Traffic Light Report

  • Moderate
    • Insight: Employees with a moderate mindset may lack motivation to go beyond basic expectations, which limits their potential and the overall impact on team performance.
    • Recommended Action: Introduce SMART goal setting (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) for clear goal alignment and encourage Stretch Assignments to push employees beyond their comfort zone, helping them reach higher levels of performance.

  • Surviving
    • Insight: Employees in survival mode often feel overwhelmed and disconnected, leading to low engagement, burnout, and decreased productivity.
    • Recommended Action: Implement Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR) programs to help employees manage stress, and introduce Job Crafting exercises to enable them to align their roles with personal strengths and interests.

Daily Reports (Department Wise, Example: Sales Department)

Sales Department Focus Tracker

  • Score greater than 55 but less than 70
    • Insight: Employees with moderate focus may complete tasks but often lack consistent attention, impacting overall productivity and project outcomes.
    • Recommended Action: Encourage time-blocking techniques and provide regular check-ins to maintain focus and drive consistency.

  • Score greater than 30 but less than 55
    • Insight: Employees with low focus may struggle with task completion and miss deadlines, leading to inefficiencies and reduced team performance.
    • Recommended Action: Implement structured work schedules with clear priorities and teach techniques (e.g., Pomodoro method) to improve sustained attention.

Sales Department Sentiment Tracker

  • Angry
    • Insight: Employees displaying anger may disrupt team dynamics and communication, leading to conflict, lower morale, and diminished productivity.
    • Recommended Action: Offer conflict resolution training and encourage regular emotional check-ins to help employees express concerns constructively and manage their emotions effectively.

  • Depressed
    • Insight: Depressed employees often experience reduced motivation, low engagement, and difficulty meeting deadlines, which can significantly impact team performance and well-being.
    • Recommended Action: Provide flexible work options to alleviate pressure and Create a culture of psychological safety where employees feel comfortable seeking help without stigma.
