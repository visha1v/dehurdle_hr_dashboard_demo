Monthly Reports (Department Wise, Example: Sales Department)

Sales Department Wrecker Report

  • Overthinker
    • Insight: Employees who overthink tend to delay decision-making, leading to inefficiencies and missed opportunities, ultimately affecting productivity and team morale.
    • Recommended Action: Implement decision-making frameworks to help employees act quickly and confidently.

  • Pleaser
    • Insight: Employees who excessively please others may struggle with setting boundaries, leading to burnout and resentment, and ultimately decreasing overall team performance and well-being.
    • Recommended Action: Train managers to foster an environment of psychological safety where employees feel comfortable expressing their needs and setting healthy boundaries.
